# Image Specifications for Walking to Car Demo

## Required Image

**Filename:** `walking-to-car.png`
**Location:** `/client/public/images/walking-to-car.png`

### Specifications

**Dimensions:**
- Width: 350-400px
- Height: 200-250px
- Aspect Ratio: Approximately 4:3

**Format:**
- PNG with transparent background (preferred)
- Or PNG/JPG with white or light gray background

**Content:**
- Person walking from left to right
- Suggests movement/progress toward a car
- Professional but friendly style (matches site's warm, approachable aesthetic)
- Consider showing 2-3 positions/frames if you want subtle animation effect

**Style Notes:**
- Match the site's warm orange/amber color palette
- Could be illustration, minimalist, or semi-realistic
- Avoid too much detail - focus on the concept of "walking to car"
- Should work well at small sizes (will be displayed in a 350x250px container)
