## ROI & Productivity Research

### 1. McKinsey Global Institute (2023)
**Title:** The Economic Potential of Generative AI: The Next Productivity Frontier
**URL:** https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-digital/our-insights/the-economic-potential-of-generative-ai-the-next-productivity-frontier
**Published:** June 14, 2023
**Authority:** McKinsey & Company (global consulting firm)
**Key Findings:**
- Generative AI could add $2.6-4.4 trillion annually to global economy
- Customer operations: 30-45% productivity improvement potential
- Software development: 20-45% productivity improvement
- Knowledge work automation across functions

**Agent Shift Use:** Supports time savings estimates in 50-70% range for knowledge work tasks.
